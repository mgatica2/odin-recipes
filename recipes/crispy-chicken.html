Chicken Cutlets

Description

This crispy chicken cutlets recipe takes flavor inspiration from the classic Caesar salad. The breadcrumb mixture includes crushed croutons for added flavor and crunch, plus store bought Caesar salad dressing is what adheres that crispy crust to the juicy chicken beneath. It's cooked right in the air fryer for an easy dinner as is, or slice them up to serve over a salad or in a sandwich or wrap.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup seasoned croutons
  • 1/2 cup seasoned breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup pre-shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons lemon pepper seasoning mix
  • 2 teaspoons ground oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 3/4 teaspoon garilc powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup creamy Caesar dressing
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • cooking spray
  • 6 chicken breast

Steps

  1. Gather All Ingredients
  2. Process croutons in a food processor until finely crumbled.
  3. Prepare dredging station.
  4. Preheat air fryer to 390 degreed F for 2 minutes.
  5. 1 cutlet at a time, submerge into dressing mixture. Place on plate in an even layer.
  6. Work in batches, cook until golden brown for 10-12 minutes.
  7. Transfer to serving platter, serve with additional Parmesan cheese and dressing.
